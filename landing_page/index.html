A Little About Me

I've always had an interest in things that present a challenge to me. That's why I am in the business of graphic and web design. I first started with graphic design when I began learning the program, GIMP, and made webcomics, posters and anything else that came to mind. I learned about html when the comic hosting site I used allowed you to customize your hosting pages. I was sad when they took that feature away. I got back into programming when I began attending Full Sail University and learned so much more than what I learned on my own back then. I now can use html, css, and Photoshop efficiently. I am still getting the hang of Javascript. Right now I am in the process of teaching myself Bootstrap so I know a css framework. I find enjoyment in programming because, like I said, it presents a challenge to me.
